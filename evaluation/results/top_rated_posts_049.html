I/O Example

INPUT: input1

UserId:IntAlias:Str
1MrX

1MrA

OUTPUT

UserId:IntAlias:Str
1MrX, MrY, MrA
2Abc, Xyz

Hints

Our Solution


SELECT
    UserId,
    string_agg(Alias, ', ') 
FROM
    input1 
GROUP BY
    UserId 
ORDER BY
    UserId ASC
